<p><strong>The Electric Heat Trace Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Heaters</li><li>Constant Wattage Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric heat trace cable market comprises two main types: self-regulating heaters and constant wattage heaters. Self-regulating heaters adjust their output automatically based on temperature changes, providing energy efficiency and preventing overheating. They are ideal for varying heat requirements. In contrast, constant wattage heaters deliver a fixed power output across their length, ensuring uniform heat but can overheat without proper controls. Each type serves distinct applications in industrial, commercial, and residential settings, enhancing safety and reliability in temperature maintenance.</p></p>
