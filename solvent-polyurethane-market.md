<p><p>The solvent polyurethane market is categorized into three main types: polyether, polyester, and mixed types. Polyether-type polyurethanes offer excellent flexibility and water resistance, making them ideal for applications like coatings and adhesives. Polyester-type polyurethanes provide enhanced durability and abrasion resistance, suitable for tougher environments. Mixed-type polyurethanes combine attributes of both polyether and polyester, allowing for tailored performance in various industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs in sectors such as automotive, construction, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
