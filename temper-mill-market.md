<p><p>The Temper Mill market includes various types based on the number of rolls used in the rolling process. A 2HI Temper Mill utilizes two rolls for simple thickness adjustments, while a 4HI Temper Mill features four rolls, allowing for better control and finishing of thinner materials. The 6HI Temper Mill employs six rolls for even greater precision and efficiency in processing very thin gauges. Other market types might include specialized configurations like tandem or reversible mills, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
