<p><strong>The Slip-On Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flate Face (FF)</li><li>Raised Face (RF)</li><li>Ring Type Joint (RTJ)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Slip-On Flanges market comprises three main types: Flat Face (FF), Raised Face (RF), and Ring Type Joint (RTJ). Flat Face flanges have a flat sealing surface, suitable for low-pressure applications. Raised Face flanges feature a protruding rim for better sealing under higher pressure. Ring Type Joint flanges utilize a metal ring to ensure a tight seal, making them ideal for high-pressure and temperature environments. Each type caters to specific industrial requirements, enhancing their applicability across various sectors.</p></p>
