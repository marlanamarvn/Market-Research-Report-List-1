<p><p>Fluorescent protein labeling involves tagging molecules with fluorescent markers for visualization in biological research. **Protein-based fluorophores** are genetically encoded, like GFP, offering high specificity and compatibility with live cells. **Organic fluorophores** are small synthetic dyes, providing bright signals and versatility but may require chemical conjugation. **Organic polymers** are larger, engineered for enhanced stability and brightness, often used in advanced imaging. **Other markets** include novel or hybrid labeling technologies, combining unique properties for specialized applications in microscopy, diagnostics, and drug discovery.</p></p>
