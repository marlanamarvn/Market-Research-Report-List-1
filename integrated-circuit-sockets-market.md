<p><p>The Integrated Circuit Sockets market comprises various types that serve distinct purposes. Dual In-Line Memory Module (DIMM) sockets are designed for connecting memory modules within computers, ensuring efficient data transfer. Production sockets are used during manufacturing processes for testing and programming ICs, facilitating mass production. Test/Burn-In sockets are vital for reliability testing, allowing ICs to undergo stress tests to identify defects before deployment. Each socket type plays a crucial role in the lifecycle of integrated circuits, from production to end-use.</p></p>
