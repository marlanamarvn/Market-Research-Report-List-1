<p><strong>The Clean Label Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clean Label Pectin market is categorized into two main types: High Methoxyl Pectin (HMP) and Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMP). HMP, derived from citrus fruits, requires sugar and acid for gel formation, making it suitable for jams and jellies. Conversely, LMP can gel with lower sugar content and is responsive to calcium, making it ideal for low-sugar or sugar-free products. Both types cater to consumer demand for clean label products, emphasizing natural ingredients and health consciousness.</p></p>
