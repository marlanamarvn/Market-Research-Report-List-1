<p><p>The Tuberculosis (TB) diagnostics market comprises several key types. Culture-based diagnostics involve growing TB bacteria from samples, providing a definitive diagnosis but taking time. Sputum smear microscopy enables quick detection by examining stained sputum samples under a microscope. Rapid molecular diagnostics utilize advanced techniques like PCR to identify TB DNA swiftly and accurately. Other diagnostic methods include immunological tests and imaging techniques. Each type offers different benefits in terms of speed, accuracy, and cost in identifying TB infections.</p></p>
