<p><ul><li>Torrefaction</li><li>Steam Explosion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Wood Pellets Market encompasses various production techniques, primarily torrefaction and steam explosion. Torrefaction involves heating biomass in the absence of oxygen, enhancing its energy density and improving its durability and combustion properties. Steam explosion, on the other hand, uses high-pressure steam to break down biomass structure, facilitating better pellet formation and combustion efficiency. Both methods aim to produce high-quality wood pellets that meet the rising demands for renewable energy and sustainable biomass fuel alternatives.</p></p>
