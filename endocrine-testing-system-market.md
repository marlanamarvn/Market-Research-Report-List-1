<p><p>The endocrine testing system market encompasses various technologies for analyzing hormonal levels. Tandem Mass Spectrometry (Tandem MS) allows for precise identification of hormones due to its high sensitivity and specificity. Liquid Chromatography-Tandem Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS/MS) integrates separation and analysis of complex samples. Immunoassays utilize antibodies for hormone detection, while monoclonal and polyclonal antibody technologies cater to different specificity needs. Sensor technology offers real-time monitoring, and clinical chemistry focuses on chemical analysis of bodily fluids, with “others” representing emerging or niche methods.</p></p>
