<p><p>Zinc-plated mild steel is a corrosion-resistant material coated with a thin layer of zinc to enhance durability. In the market, it is available in three primary forms: Steel Coil, Coil, and Steel Plate. Steel Coil refers to continuous, rolled sheets of zinc-plated mild steel, ideal for manufacturing and construction. Coil is a general term for wound steel sheets, often used interchangeably with Steel Coil. Steel Plate denotes flat, thicker sheets, suitable for heavy-duty applications like structural frameworks and machinery. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
