<p><p>The thermoplastic road marking machine market comprises three primary types: hand-pushed, self-propelled, and line marking trucks. Hand-pushed types are manually operated, suitable for small projects and intricate designs. Self-propelled marking machines feature built-in engines for enhanced mobility and efficiency, ideal for larger tasks. Line marking trucks, or mini trucks, are specialized vehicles equipped for extensive road marking applications, combining thermoplastic technology with fluid transportation, making them suitable for urban and highway projects, ensuring precision and speed in execution.</p></p>
