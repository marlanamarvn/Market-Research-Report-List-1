<p><p>General Ledger Software is categorized into three main types: On-Premise, Web-Based, and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s hardware, offering high control but requiring significant maintenance. Web-Based software operates through a browser, providing easier access and updates, yet may depend on internet connectivity. Cloud-Based solutions store data on remote servers, ensuring scalability and collaboration, often with subscription models, making it easier for businesses to manage their financial records without heavy upfront costs.</p></p>
