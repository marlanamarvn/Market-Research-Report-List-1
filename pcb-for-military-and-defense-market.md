<p><p>The military and defense market for PCBs encompasses three main types: rigid PCBs, flexible PCBs, and rigid-flex PCBs. Rigid PCBs provide robustness and durability for fixed applications, ideal for equipment requiring stable performance. Flexible PCBs offer lightweight, compact designs, enabling integration into complex, space-constrained environments. Rigid-flex PCBs combine the benefits of both types; they deliver versatility and mechanical stability while accommodating intricate layouts. Each type is tailored to meet the high reliability and performance standards essential in defense applications.</p></p>
