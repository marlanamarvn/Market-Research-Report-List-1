<p><strong>The Automotive Axle Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Floating Axle Parts</li><li>Semi-Floating Axle Parts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive axle parts market includes Full-Floating and Semi-Floating axle types. Full-Floating axle parts are designed to handle heavy loads, with the axle shaft solely transmitting torque while the wheel hub bears the vehicle's weight. They are commonly used in trucks and heavy-duty vehicles. Semi-Floating axle parts support both torque transmission and vehicle weight, integrating the axle shaft and hub. These are typically found in lighter vehicles like passenger cars, offering a balance of strength and cost-efficiency.</p></p>
