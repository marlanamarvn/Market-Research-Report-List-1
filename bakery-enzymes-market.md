<p><p>The bakery enzymes market comprises various types that enhance dough quality and extend shelf life. Amylase breaks down starches, improving yeast fermentation. Glucose oxidase strengthens gluten structure and increases dough strength. Lipase enhances flavor and contributes to crust characteristics. Lipoxygenase improves dough elasticity and stability. Xylanase enhances water absorption and dough texture. Protease aids in gluten breakdown, improving softness. Asparaginase reduces acrylamide formation during baking, promoting safety. Together, these enzymes elevate the overall quality of baked products.</p></p>
