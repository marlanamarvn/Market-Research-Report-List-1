<p><p>The networking cables market comprises three main types: coaxial, twisted pair, and optical fiber cables. Coaxial cables offer effective signal transmission and are commonly used for cable television and internet connections. Twisted pair cables, including unshielded and shielded variants, are widely utilized in local area networks (LANs) for data transfer due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Optical fiber cables, known for high-speed data transmission over long distances, are increasingly favored in telecommunications due to their superior bandwidth and immunity to electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
