<p><p>The infusion pump device market encompasses various product types, including volumetric infusion pumps, which deliver precise fluid volumes, and syringe infusion pumps that administer medications in a controlled manner. Insulin infusion pumps help manage diabetes, while ambulatory infusion pumps allow patients to receive treatment on the go. Enteral infusion pumps provide nutrition directly to the gastrointestinal tract. Patient-controlled analgesia (PCA) infusion pumps enable patients to self-administer pain relief. Lastly, implantable infusion pumps are designed for long-term medication delivery within the body. The large volume pumps category caters to intensive care settings requiring significant fluid administration.</p></p>
