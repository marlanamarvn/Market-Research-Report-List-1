<p><p>The demineralization systems market is categorized into semi-automatic and automatic types. Semi-automatic systems require manual intervention for operations such as regenerating ion exchange resins, making them more flexible and cost-effective for smaller applications. In contrast, automatic systems enhance efficiency by automating processes like monitoring, regeneration, and operation, reducing the need for human oversight. This automation results in consistent water quality, lower operational costs, and increased reliability, making automatic systems suitable for larger industrial applications and continuous operations.</p></p>
