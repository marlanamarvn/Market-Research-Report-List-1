<p><p>The hair trimmer market comprises various types based on motor technology and functionality. Magnetic motor types use electrical magnets for efficient cutting, ideal for fine hair. Pivot motor types utilize a two-pole design for superior torque, suitable for thick hair. Rotary motor types offer versatile power and speed, accommodating different hair types and lengths. Manual types, less common, rely on manual operation for precise trimming, typically used for detail work or travel. Each type caters to specific user needs and preferences.</p></p>
