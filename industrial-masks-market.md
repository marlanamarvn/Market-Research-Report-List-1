<p><ul><li>Valveless Mask</li><li>Breather Valve Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial masks market features two primary types: valveless masks and breather valve masks. Valveless masks are designed without any breathing valves, providing a simple barrier for pollutants, ideal for low-risk environments. In contrast, breather valve masks incorporate valves that enhance airflow, making them more suitable for prolonged use in demanding conditions, as they allow exhaled air to escape easily, reducing heat and moisture buildup. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring worker safety in various industrial settings.</p></p>
