<p><p>The aerospace hydraulic motors market is primarily segmented into aerospace bent hydraulic motors and aerospace inline hydraulic motors. In contrast, inline hydraulic motors feature a straight shaft design, providing straightforward installation and maintenance. Both types play crucial roles in actuator systems, landing gear, and control surfaces, enabling precise movement and reliable operation in aerospace applications.</p></p>
