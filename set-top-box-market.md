<p><strong>The Set-Top-Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cable</li><li>Satellite</li><li>DTT</li><li>IP</li><li>OTT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Set-Top-Box (STB) market comprises several types, each catering to different delivery methods for television content. Cable STBs connect to cable networks, enabling access to a wide range of channels. Satellite STBs receive signals from satellites, providing services over vast areas. Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) STBs decode broadcasts transmitted via terrestrial towers. Internet Protocol (IP) STBs deliver content over broadband connections, while Over-The-Top (OTT) STBs stream content directly from the internet, bypassing traditional delivery systems for more flexible viewing options.</p></p>
