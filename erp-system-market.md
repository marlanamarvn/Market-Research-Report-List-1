<p><p>The ERP system market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ERP systems are hosted on vendors' servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They are ideal for remote access and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises ERP systems are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Organizations choose based on their specific needs and resources.</p></p>
