<p><p>The radial piston pump market includes various types that enhance efficiency and performance. Cylinder type pumps are characterized by their cylindrical pistons arranged radially, providing high pressure and flow rates. Crankshaft linkage pumps utilize a crankshaft to convert rotational motion into linear motion for piston movement, offering reliability and smooth operation. Polygonal transmission pumps employ a unique polygon shape to facilitate piston movement, enhancing torque and reducing wear. Each type serves specific industrial applications, emphasizing versatility and innovation in fluid handling.</p></p>
