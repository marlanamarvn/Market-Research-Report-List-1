<p><p>The UV LED curing market is categorized into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs, typically used for curing inks and coatings, emit wavelengths between 320-400 nm, providing effective polymerization. UV-B LEDs, with wavelengths between 280-320 nm, are often used in medical and therapeutic applications. UV-C LEDs, emitting wavelengths below 280 nm, are primarily used for disinfection and sterilization purposes. Each type serves distinct applications based on their wavelength characteristics and energy output.</p></p>
