<p><strong>The Nuclear Power Reactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR)</li><li>Boiling Water Reactor (BWR)</li><li>Pressurized Heavy Water Reactor (PHWR)</li><li>Fast Breeder Reactor (FBR)</li><li>Light Water Graphite Reactor (LWR)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear power reactors market comprises several key types. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use pressurized water to transfer heat, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) boil water to produce steam directly. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) utilize heavy water as a moderator, enhancing fuel efficiency. Fast Breeder Reactors (FBR) convert non-fissile material into fissile fuel, maximizing fuel use. Light Water Graphite Reactors (LWR) employ graphite as a moderator for thermal activity. Additionally, there are other reactor designs catering to specific needs and technologies.</p></p>
