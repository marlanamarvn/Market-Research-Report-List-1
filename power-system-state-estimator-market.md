<p><strong>The Power System State Estimator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weighted Lease Square (WLS) Method</li><li>Interior Point (IP) Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In power system state estimation, market types include the Weighted Least Squares (WLS) method, which minimizes the weighted sum of squared errors, effectively balancing measurement accuracy. The Interior Point (IP) method, on the other hand, solves optimization problems by traversing the interior of feasible regions, offering improved convergence for large-scale systems. Other methods may include traditional least squares and hybrid approaches, aiming to efficiently estimate system states while accommodating various constraints and measurement types in power markets.</p></p>
