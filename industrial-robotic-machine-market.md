<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Parallel Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Cylindrical Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robotic machine market comprises various types of robots, each designed for specific applications. Articulated robots feature rotating joints, providing flexibility for complex tasks. Parallel robots have arms connected to a base, offering high precision and stability for assembly tasks. SCARA robots excel in horizontal movements, ideal for pick-and-place operations. Cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear motions, suitable for packaging and material handling. Cartesian robots use linear movements along three axes, commonly used in assembly and CNC machining applications.</p></p>
