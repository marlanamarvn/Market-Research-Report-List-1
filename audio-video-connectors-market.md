<p><ul><li>Analog AV Connectors</li><li>Digital AV Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio and video connectors market is divided into two main types: analog AV connectors and digital AV connectors. Analog AV connectors, such as RCA, VGA, and 3.5mm jacks, transmit signals in continuous form, making them suitable for legacy devices and simpler applications. In contrast, digital AV connectors, including HDMI, DisplayPort, and USB-C, provide higher quality, multi-channel audio and video transmission with advanced features like 4K resolution support. Both types cater to different consumer needs and technological advancements in multimedia.</p></p>
