<p><p>The fermented bean curd market primarily consists of two types: white preserved bean curd and red fermented bean curd. White preserved bean curd is typically made from soybeans and has a mild flavor, often used in marinades or as a condiment. In contrast, red fermented bean curd is infused with red yeast rice, giving it a distinct color and slightly tangy taste, making it popular in various Asian cuisines. Both types cater to diverse culinary applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
