<p><p>The UAV market for aerial photography is primarily divided into two types: rotary wing UAVs and fixed wing UAVs. Rotary wing UAVs, commonly known as drones, are highly maneuverable and ideal for capturing detailed images in tight spaces or complex terrains. They take off and land vertically, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, fixed wing UAVs are designed for longer flight durations and covering larger areas.
