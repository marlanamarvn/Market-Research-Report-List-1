<p><strong>The Mist-free Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Humidifier</li><li>Evaporative Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mist-free humidifier market primarily features two types: ultrasonic and evaporative humidifiers. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to produce a fine mist without heat, offering quiet operation and energy efficiency. They are ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, evaporative humidifiers rely on a fan to blow air through a moistened wick or filter, promoting natural evaporation. This type is self-regulating, preventing over-humidification. Together, they cater to diverse consumer preferences for effective indoor humidity management.</p></p>
