<p><p>Residential Energy Recovery Ventilation (ERV) units are categorized into three main market types: wall-mount, ceiling-mount, and cabinet-mount. Wall-mount ERVs are designed for easy installation on the wall, making them ideal for tight spaces. Ceiling-mount units are integrated into the ceiling for a discreet option, suitable for larger areas. Cabinet-mount ERVs are enclosed in cabinets, often used in utility rooms, providing centralized ventilation with a compact design. Each type serves specific installation needs and space constraints while enhancing indoor air quality.</p></p>
