<p><p>The lung laryngeal stents market is categorized into three primary types: </p><p>1. **Self-expandable Stents**: Designed to expand automatically upon placement, these stents offer consistent airway support and are often made of flexible materials.</p><p>2. **Balloon-expandable Stents**: These require a balloon to be inflated for expansion, allowing for precise placement and a more rigid structure once deployed.</p><p>3. **Non-expandable Stents**: Fixed in size, these stents provide a stable airway but lack the adjustability of the other types, making them suitable for specific clinical needs.</p></p>
