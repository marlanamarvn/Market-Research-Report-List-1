<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound diagnostic instrument market comprises primarily two types: Coronary Intravascular Ultrasound and Peripheral Intravascular Ultrasound. Coronary intravascular ultrasound focuses on imaging the coronary arteries to assess and diagnose conditions like atherosclerosis and guide interventions. Conversely, peripheral intravascular ultrasound targets the peripheral vascular system, aiding in diagnosing issues such as peripheral artery disease. Both types enhance visualization, enabling accurate assessments and improved clinical outcomes in cardiovascular procedures while providing critical information that influences treatment strategies.</p></p>
