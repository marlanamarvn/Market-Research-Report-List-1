<p><p>Multi-layer flexible packaging encompasses various market types, including top loading and bottom loading systems. Top loading packaging involves items being inserted from the top, often utilizing a flap or sliding mechanism, making them ideal for quick access and efficient filling. In contrast, bottom loading packaging enables items to be inserted from the bottom, which can enhance stability and optimize shelf space. Both methods offer distinct benefits, catering to different product types and consumer preferences in the flexible packaging market.</p></p>
