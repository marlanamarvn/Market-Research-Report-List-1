<p><p>The pediatric cranial remodeling helmet market is segmented into four key types based on head shape abnormalities. Plagiocephaly involves a flat spot on one side of the head, brachycephaly is characterized by a broad, short skull, scaphocephaly features a long, narrow head shape, and craniosynostosis refers to the premature fusion of skull sutures, affecting skull growth. Each condition requires specific helmets designed to reshape and correct head formation in infants, promoting healthy cranial development.</p></p>
