<p><p>The industrial-use nitrile gloves market is primarily segmented into two types: powder-free and powdered nitrile gloves. Powder-free nitrile gloves are designed to eliminate the risk of powder-related allergies and contamination, making them suitable for medical and food handling applications. In contrast, powdered nitrile gloves contain cornstarch powder that facilitates easier donning and doffing, making them popular in less sensitive environments. Both types offer excellent chemical resistance and durability, catering to various industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and food processing.</p></p>
