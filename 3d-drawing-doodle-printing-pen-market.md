<p><p>The 3D Drawing Doodle Printing Pen market primarily features two types: Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) 3D Printing Pens and Stereolithography (SLA) 3D Printing Pens. FDM pens extrude heated plastic filaments to create three-dimensional objects layer by layer, ideal for beginners due to their ease of use. In contrast, SLA pens utilize a UV light to cure resin, producing highly detailed and smooth designs, making them suitable for advanced users who prioritize precision. Both cater to different user preferences and applications.</p></p>
