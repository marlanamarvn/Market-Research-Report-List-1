<p><p>The Neo Magnet market can be categorized into two primary types: bonding and sintering. Bonding involves the adhesion of magnetic powders using adhesives or resins, allowing for complex shapes and reduced costs. This method is advantageous for specific applications requiring flexibility and precision. Sintering, on the other hand, involves compacting and heating powdered metals to create dense, high-performance magnets. This method typically offers superior magnetic properties and durability, making it ideal for demanding applications in various industries.</p></p>
