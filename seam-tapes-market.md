<p><p>The seam tapes market is primarily categorized into two types: polyurethane (PU) and poly vinyl chloride (PVC) tapes. Polyurethane seam tapes are known for their flexibility, durability, and moisture resistance, making them ideal for outdoor and high-performance applications, especially in the clothing and technical textiles sectors. On the other hand, poly vinyl chloride seam tapes offer strong adhesion and are often utilized in industries such as construction and packaging for sealing and waterproofing tasks. Both materials serve distinct functional needs in various markets.</p></p>
