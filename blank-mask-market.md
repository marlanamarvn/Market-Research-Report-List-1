<p><strong>The Blank Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Blank Mask</li><li>Attenuated Phase Shift Blank Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blank masks are vital in photolithography, used for semiconductor manufacturing. Low Reflectance Chrome-film Blank Masks minimize light reflection, enhancing image clarity during exposure. They are ideal for high-resolution circuits. Attenuated Phase Shift Blank Masks, on the other hand, utilize phase-shifting materials to improve diffraction patterns, leading to better feature definition in advanced lithography processes. Both types play crucial roles in the production of integrated circuits, enabling the creation of smaller, more intricate components with improved performance.</p></p>
