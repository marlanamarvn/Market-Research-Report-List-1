<p><p>The Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS) microphones market is primarily segmented into analog and digital types. Analog MEMS microphones convert sound waves into continuous voltage signals, making them simple and cost-effective, suitable for basic applications. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones provide output in a digital format, typically using Pulse Density Modulation or I2S interfaces. They offer advanced features like noise cancellation and enhanced sound quality, making them ideal for consumer electronics, smartphones, and other high-performance audio devices.</p></p>
