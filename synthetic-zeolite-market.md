<p><p>The Synthetic Zeolite market includes various types like A-type, X-type, Y-type, and others. A-type zeolites are widely used in detergents for water softening due to their high ion-exchange capacity. X-type zeolites are utilized in gas separation and adsorption processes, offering excellent selectivity. Y-type zeolites are crucial in petroleum refining and catalytic cracking due to their stability and acidity. Other zeolites cater to niche applications like environmental remediation, agriculture, and specialty chemicals, showcasing the versatility of synthetic zeolites across industries.</p></p>
