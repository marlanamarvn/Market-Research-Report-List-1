<p><p>Valve actuator systems are critical for controlling fluid flow in various industries. They come in several types: </p><p>- **Manual actuators** require human effort for operation, ideal for low-frequency tasks. </p><p>- **Pneumatic actuators** use compressed air for fast action and are suited for high-speed applications. </p><p>- **Hydraulic actuators** utilize pressurized fluid for powerful performance, excellent for heavy-duty tasks. </p><p>- **Electric actuators** offer precise control and automation, suitable for complex systems. </p><p>- **Spring actuators** rely on stored energy for fail-safe operations, ensuring reliability in emergencies.</p></p>
