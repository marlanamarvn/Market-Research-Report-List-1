<p><p>The Automotive Oil Pressure Control Valves market includes various types designed to regulate oil flow and pressure in vehicles. **Sliding-Stem Globe Type** valves use a linear motion stem to control flow, offering precise regulation. **V-Notch Ball Type** valves feature a ball with a V-shaped notch for accurate flow modulation. **Butterfly Type** valves use a rotating disc for quick shut-off and flow control. **Angle Type** valves direct flow at an angle, ideal for tight spaces and high-pressure systems. Each type caters to specific automotive applications.</p></p>
