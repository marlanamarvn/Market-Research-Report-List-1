<p><p>The pillow packing machine market is segmented into horizontal and vertical types, each designed for specific packaging needs. Horizontal pillow packing machines operate with products laid flat, ideal for items like snacks and granola bars, ensuring efficient sealing and presentation. In contrast, vertical pillow packing machines operate with products fed from above, suitable for liquids or small granules, providing compactness and versatility. Both types enhance productivity and maintain product integrity while catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
