<p><p>The car essential oil diffuser market features three primary types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Car Diffuser**: Utilizes ultrasonic waves to create a fine mist of essential oils and water, providing a soothing aroma while also humidifying the air.</p><p>2. **Electric Heat Car Diffuser**: Heats essential oils to release their fragrance, offering a consistent scent without the need for water.</p><p>3. **Evaporated Car Diffuser**: Relies on airflow to disperse essential oils, using reeds or pads that absorb the oil and allow it to evaporate into the air, providing a simple and natural fragrance solution.</p></p>
