<p><p>The antibacterial therapeutic market is segmented into various classes based on their chemical structure and mechanism of action. Monobactams and carbapenems are also beta-lactams, known for their broad-spectrum efficacy. Macrolides target bacterial protein synthesis, and other categories may include newer agents or combination therapies. This diversity ensures tailored treatment options for bacterial infections.</p></p>
