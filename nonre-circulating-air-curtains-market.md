<p><p>The non-recirculating air curtains market features three primary installation types: horizontal, vertical, and recessed. Horizontal installations are typically mounted above entryways, creating a barrier against air exchange while allowing easy access. Vertical installations, often used for larger openings, provide effective separation by directing airflow downward along the sides. Recessed air curtains are discreetly integrated into ceilings or walls, offering a clean aesthetic while maintaining functionality. Each type is designed to enhance energy efficiency and improve indoor comfort in various commercial environments.</p></p>
