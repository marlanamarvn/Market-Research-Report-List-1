<p><p>Mono PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) solar cells are a type of solar technology that improves the efficiency of traditional monocrystalline solar cells. By adding a layer of passivation on the rear side, these cells reduce electron recombination and enhance light absorption, leading to increased energy output. The demand for Mono PERC solar cells has surged due to their superior efficiency and performance in various weather conditions, making them a preferred choice among solar developers and homeowners.</p><p>The Mono PERC Solar Cells Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period.
