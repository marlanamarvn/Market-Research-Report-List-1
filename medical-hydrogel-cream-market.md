<p><ul><li>Physical Crosslinked Hydrogels</li><li>Crosslinking Agent Crosslinked Hydrogels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical hydrogel cream market includes various types based on their structure and composition. Physical crosslinked hydrogels utilize non-covalent bonds, allowing for flexibility and responsiveness to environmental changes, making them ideal for wound dressings and drug delivery. In contrast, crosslinking agent crosslinked hydrogels are formed using chemical crosslinkers, resulting in stable networks that provide enhanced mechanical strength and controlled release properties. Both types are essential in healthcare applications, offering unique benefits tailored to specific medical needs.</p></p>
