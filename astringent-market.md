<p><p>The astringent market comprises various types, each serving specific medical needs. Combination astringents blend multiple agents for enhanced efficacy. Gelatin-based astringents offer biocompatibility and absorb excess blood, while collagen-based astringents facilitate tissue healing and regeneration. Together, these diverse astringents cater to different surgical environments, enhancing patient outcomes through effective hemorrhage control and tissue management.</p></p>
