<p><strong>The RTD Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Wire</li><li>3 Wire</li><li>4 Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTD cables are essential for temperature sensing applications, categorized by their wire configuration. </p><p>- **2 Wire RTDs** offer basic functionality, connecting the sensor directly to the measurement device, but are prone to errors due to lead resistance. </p><p>- **3 Wire RTDs** improve accuracy by compensating for one lead's resistance, enhancing measurement reliability. </p><p>- **4 Wire RTDs** provide the highest precision, eliminating lead resistance errors entirely by using two leads for current supply and two for measurement, making them ideal for critical applications.</p></p>
