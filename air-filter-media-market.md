<p><p>The Air Filter Media market includes HEPA Grade Media, MERV Grade Media, and ULPA Grade Media, each serving distinct filtration needs. HEPA Grade Media captures 99.97% of particles ≥0.3 microns, ideal for hospitals and cleanrooms. MERV Grade Media offers varying efficiency levels (1-16), suitable for residential and commercial HVAC systems. ULPA Grade Media surpasses HEPA, trapping 99.999% of particles ≥0.12 microns, used in ultra-clean environments like semiconductor manufacturing. Each type balances efficiency, airflow resistance, and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
