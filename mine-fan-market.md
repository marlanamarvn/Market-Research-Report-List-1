<p><p>The mine fan market is categorized into three types based on pressure levels: low pressure fans, medium pressure fans, and high pressure fans. Low pressure fans are typically used for general ventilation in mines, providing sufficient airflow at lower resistance. Medium pressure fans offer a balance between airflow and pressure, suitable for applications requiring more robust ventilation. High pressure fans are designed for demanding environments, delivering substantial airflow against high resistance, crucial for deep mines or areas with complex ventilation needs.</p></p>
