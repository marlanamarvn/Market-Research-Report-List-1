<p><ul><li>Direct Operated</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Variable Displacement Vane Pump market is segmented into Direct Operated and Pilot Operated types. Direct Operated pumps utilize a mechanical linkage to adjust displacement, providing immediate response to changes in demand. In contrast, Pilot Operated pumps use a secondary control system to govern displacement, offering enhanced precision and efficiency in response to pressure variations. Both types cater to diverse applications, with Direct Operated pumps suited for straightforward systems and Pilot Operated pumps ideal for more complex hydraulic circuits.</p></p>
