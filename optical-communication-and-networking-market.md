<p><p>The optical communication and networking market includes several key components: </p><p>1. **Optical Fibers** facilitate high-speed data transmission over long distances through light signals. </p><p>2. **Optical Transceivers** convert electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa, enabling bi-directional communication. </p><p>3. **Optical Amplifiers** boost signal strength over long distances, enhancing transmission efficiency. </p><p>4. **Optical Switches** control data routing within networks, ensuring efficient traffic management. </p><p>5. **Optical Splitters** distribute optical signals to multiple outputs. </p><p>6. **Optical Circulators** direct light signals in a specific order, enhancing signal integrity and network performance.</p></p>
