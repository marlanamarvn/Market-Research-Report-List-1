<p><strong>The Waste-to-Energy Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Technologies</li><li>Biochemical Reactions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waste-to-Energy technologies encompass Thermal Technologies and Biochemical Reactions. Thermal Technologies, such as incineration and gasification, convert waste into energy through high-temperature processes, producing electricity and heat while reducing landfill volume. In contrast, Biochemical Reactions involve anaerobic digestion and fermentation, where microorganisms break down organic waste to generate biogas, which can be used for energy production. Both methods offer sustainable solutions for waste management, enabling energy recovery and contributing to circular economy initiatives by transforming waste into valuable resources.</p></p>
