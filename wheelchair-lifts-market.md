<p><p>The wheelchair lifts market primarily features two types: hydraulic wheelchair lifts and electric wheelchair lifts. Hydraulic wheelchair lifts utilize fluid pressure to raise and lower the platform, offering smooth operation and high lifting capacity, making them ideal for heavy-duty uses. In contrast, electric wheelchair lifts rely on electric motors for movement, providing a more compact and energy-efficient option suitable for residential and commercial settings. Both types enhance accessibility, ensuring individuals with mobility challenges can navigate different levels with ease.</p></p>
