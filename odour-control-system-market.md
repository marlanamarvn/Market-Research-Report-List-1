<p><p>Odour control systems are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Physical Odour Control** involves methods like activated carbon filters and air scrubbers to physically remove or capture odorous compounds from the air. </p><p>2. **Biological Odour Control** utilizes microorganisms to break down and neutralize odorous substances, often implemented in treatment facilities. </p><p>3. **Chemical Odour Control** employs chemical agents to neutralize or mask unpleasant smells, commonly found in various industrial applications. Each type addresses odour issues through distinct mechanisms, catering to diverse environments.</p></p>
