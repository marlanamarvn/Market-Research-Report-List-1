<p><p>The Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) market comprises three primary types: Real-Time PCR, Traditional PCR, and Digital PCR. Real-Time PCR enables the simultaneous amplification and quantification of DNA, offering rapid results. Traditional PCR, the most basic form, focuses on DNA amplification without real-time monitoring, making it cost-effective but less efficient. Digital PCR provides precise measurements by isolating individual DNA molecules, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and forensics.</p></p>
