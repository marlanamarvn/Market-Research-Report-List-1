<p><p>The hydrolyzed infant formula market is divided into two main types: completely hydrolyzed and partially hydrolyzed formulas. Completely hydrolyzed formulas consist of proteins that are broken down into smaller peptides, making them suitable for infants with severe allergies or digestive issues. In contrast, partially hydrolyzed formulas contain proteins that are moderately broken down, offering a balance between digestibility and nutritional value, catering to infants with mild allergies or those at risk of developing them. Both types aim to promote safe feeding experiences for infants.</p></p>
