<p><p>The medical laser fiber market is divided into two main types: single-use and reusable medical laser fibers. Single-use fibers are designed for one-time procedures, offering advantages in terms of sterility and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for environments that prioritize patient safety. Reusable fibers, on the other hand, can be sterilized and used multiple times, offering cost-efficiency for healthcare facilities. Both types play crucial roles in various medical applications, enhancing treatment effectiveness and patient outcomes.</p></p>
