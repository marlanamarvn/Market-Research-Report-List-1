<p><p>The dough conditioners market is categorized into three main types: powder, liquid, and paste. Powder conditioners are widely used for their easy incorporation into dry ingredients, enhancing dough stability and texture. Liquid conditioners provide better dispersion and rapid absorption, making them suitable for high-moisture doughs. Paste conditioners offer a concentrated format, allowing for precise application and improved dough handling. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing baking performance, influencing the final product's quality and shelf life.</p></p>
