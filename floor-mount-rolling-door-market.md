<p><p>The floor-mount rolling door market consists of two primary types: manual and electric. Manual rolling doors require physical effort to operate, typically utilizing a locking mechanism and a hand crank or pull-down system, making them suitable for lower-traffic areas or budget-conscious users. Electric rolling doors, on the other hand, are powered by motors, offering convenience and ease of use, especially in high-traffic environments. These automated systems enhance security and access control while reducing the physical strain on operators.</p></p>
