<p><p>The musk market comprises three main types: Natural Musk, Artificial Musk, and Synthetic Musk. Natural Musk is derived from animal sources, specifically the musk deer, and is prized for its unique scent but is now rare and often regulated. Artificial Musk, commonly sourced from plant materials, mimics the scent of natural musk but may lack its complexity. Synthetic Musk is chemically engineered to recreate musk fragrances, offering consistency and abundance, making it widely used in perfumes and cosmetics due to its cost-effectiveness and ethical considerations.</p></p>
