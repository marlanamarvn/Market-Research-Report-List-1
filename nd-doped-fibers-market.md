<p><strong>The Nd Doped Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Clad Fiber</li><li>Double Clad Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nd-doped fibers are categorized into two primary types: single-clad fibers and double-clad fibers. Single-clad fibers have a simple structure with one cladding layer, providing efficient laser performance but limited pump light absorption. In contrast, double-clad fibers feature an additional outer cladding, allowing for better confinement of pump light and enhanced efficiency in delivering power. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements, including efficiency, output power, and operational flexibility in laser systems.</p></p>
