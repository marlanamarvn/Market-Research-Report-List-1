<p><p>The impression trays market is segmented into two main types: those for partial dentures and those for complete dentures. Impression trays for partial dentures are designed to capture detailed impressions of specific teeth and soft tissues, allowing for precise fit and function. In contrast, trays for complete dentures cover the entire arch, ensuring accurate impressions of both upper and lower jaws for optimal denture fabrication. Each type caters to specific dental restoration needs, influencing material choice and design accordingly.</p></p>
