<p><p>Payroll outsourcing involves different market types, primarily categorized as full-managed and co-managed outsourcing. Full-managed outsourcing refers to a complete transfer of payroll responsibilities to an external provider, encompassing all aspects from processing to compliance. In contrast, co-managed outsourcing involves a shared approach where the organization retains some payroll functions while collaborating with an external provider for specific tasks, such as regulatory compliance or reporting. Both models aim to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and ensure accuracy in payroll management.</p></p>
