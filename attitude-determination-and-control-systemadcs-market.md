<p><p>The Attitude Determination and Control System (ADCS) is essential for maintaining the orientation of spacecraft and satellites in orbit. It involves the use of sensors and actuators to determine and control the vehicle's attitude, ensuring it maintains the desired position for tasks such as communications, imaging, and scientific missions. The primary components of ADCS include gyroscopes, magnetometers, star trackers, and reaction wheels, which work together to ensure precise maneuverability.</p><p>The ADCS market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced satellite systems, miniaturization of technology, and the expansion of commercial space activities.
