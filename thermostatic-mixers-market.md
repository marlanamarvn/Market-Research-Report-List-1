<p><p>The thermostatic mixers market features two primary types of valve cores: wax element thermostatic valve cores and shape memory alloys (SMA) thermostatic valve cores. Wax element thermostatic valves utilize a wax-based mechanism that expands or contracts with temperature changes, efficiently regulating water flow. In contrast, shape memory alloys leverage materials that change properties according to temperature, providing precise control over water mixing. Both technologies enhance efficiency and comfort in applications like plumbing and heating systems, catering to varying consumer needs.</p></p>
