<p><p>The blown film machine market is classified into several types based on the materials processed. The PE film blowing machine produces polyethylene films, commonly used for packaging and retail purposes. The PP film blowing machine focuses on polypropylene films, known for their strength and versatility. PVC film blowing machines create polyvinyl chloride films, often utilized in construction and insulation. Lastly, PET film blowing machines manufacture polyethylene terephthalate films, valued for their clarity and durability, especially in packaging applications.</p></p>
