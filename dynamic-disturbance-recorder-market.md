<p><strong>The Dynamic Disturbance Recorder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Dynamic Disturbance Recorder</li><li>Triggered Dynamic Disturbance Recorder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dynamic Disturbance Recorder market is categorized into two main types: Continuous Dynamic Disturbance Recorders and Triggered Dynamic Disturbance Recorders. Continuous recorders operate tirelessly, capturing data on system disturbances as they occur, providing a comprehensive overview of electrical system performance. In contrast, triggered recorders capture specific events once a disturbance exceeds a predefined threshold, allowing for targeted analysis of significant electrical anomalies. Together, these devices enhance system reliability and facilitate troubleshooting in power networks.</p></p>
