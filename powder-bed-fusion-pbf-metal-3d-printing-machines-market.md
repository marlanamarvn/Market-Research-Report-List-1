<p><p>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF) metal 3D printing machines are categorized into single laser and multiple laser types based on their laser configuration. Single laser machines use one laser source to selectively melt powdered metal, offering simplicity and lower cost but slower production speeds. In contrast, multiple laser systems employ multiple lasers simultaneously, enhancing the speed and efficiency of the printing process. This allows for faster build times and larger parts, catering to high-volume production and complex geometries in metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
