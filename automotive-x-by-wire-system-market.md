<p><p>The Automotive X-by-Wire System refers to the replacement of traditional mechanical and hydraulic control systems in vehicles with electronic systems. This technology enables functions like steering, braking, and throttle control to be managed through electronic signals rather than physical linkages. Key components include sensors, actuators, and electronic control units (ECUs), which enhance vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency. X-by-wire systems are integral to the development of autonomous and electric vehicles, offering improved precision, reduced weight, and better integration with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).</p><p>The Automotive X-by-Wire System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period.
